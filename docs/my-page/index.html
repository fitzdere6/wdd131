The Mission

Bulletproof Basics exists to make shooter safety easy to understand and simple to apply. Whether someone is brand new to firearms or just wants to refresh their knowledge, this site breaks down every skill into clear, approachable steps. No politics, no pressure—just practical safety.

On this site you will learn the four rules of firearm safety, proper handling and storage, safe range etiquette, situational awareness, and basic gear for safe shooting.
